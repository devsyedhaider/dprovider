Digital Marketing FAQs

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites to promote products or services to targeted audiences.

How can SEO help my business?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves your website's visibility on search engines, driving more organic traffic and increasing your chances of attracting potential customers.

What is the difference between SEO and SEM?

SEO focuses on optimizing your site for organic search, while SEM (Search Engine Marketing) involves paid advertising strategies like Google Ads to increase visibility.

Why is social media marketing important?

Social media marketing helps you connect with your audience, build brand awareness, and drive website traffic. It allows for targeted advertising and engagement with potential customers.

How do I measure the success of a digital marketing campaign?

Success can be measured using KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) such as website traffic, conversion rates, click-through rates, and ROI (Return on Investment).
